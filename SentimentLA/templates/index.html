What is EmoSent?

The EmoSent analyzes the emotion and attitude expressed in a block of text or text conversation in social media for education. Currently, EmoSent only works for analyzing “Knowledge Forum” posts. By digging deeper into emotion and sentiment elements, the tool uncovers more context from students’ conversations and helps educators analyze students’ learning.

EmoSent analyzes text based on emotion and sentiment score;

Currently, only works for the "Knowledge Forum" posts written in English.

The results from the analysis are categorized in different visualization formats.

Who can use this tool?

Emosent aim to provide a better understanding of students’ attitude and emotion for the teachers. As teacher know about their students’ feelings they can better work on improving the content and form of instruction to enhance learning process and outcomes.
